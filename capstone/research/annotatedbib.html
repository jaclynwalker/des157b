Davis Centennial Seal

The design of the Davis Centennial Seal is inspired by the history of the city of Davis. The seal tells a story through its illustrations, showcasing the themes that are an integral part of the Davis community. These themes include aspiration, community, cooperation, leadership, innovation, engagement, global citizenship, stewardship, vision, optimism. The central image of the seal shows the geographical crossroads of Davis. The inner ring illustrates the early history of the area of Davis. The second ring tells the story of the founding of Davisville. The intersecting and radiating panels illustrate the themes of the Davis seal. The outer ring features the animals of Putah Creek.

“Davis Centennial Seal.” City of Davis, 2023, https://www.cityofdavis.org/city-hall/city-manager-s-office/arts-culture/davis-centennial-seal.

Overview of Davis Centennial Seal

The Davis Centennial Seal is a 6 ½ foot bronze art piece commemorating the history of Davis. Each ring of the seal has a different theme that is represented by a variety of illustrations. The goal of the seal is to not only honor the history of the founding of the city of Davis, but to also honor the nature and the people that existed on the land prior to the city’s founding. Each piece of the ring tells a story and embodies the themes that have been integral in the creation of the community that exists today. The seal takes a multifaceted approach to Davis history; it honors the positive accomplishments and recognizes the tragedies of the past that have shaped Davis. The seal aims to represent Davis as a proud, forward thinking city.

Shelton, Susan. “Overview of Davis Centennial Seal.” Susan Shelton Ceramic Artist, 2021, https://susanshelton.com/Seals/Davis-Centennial-Seal/Overview-of-Davis-Centennial-Seal.

Interactive Websites: Improve Your Site’s Engagement

Building an interactive website can help users enjoy your website more as you deliver your content. It is important to think strategically in order to most effectively deliver content with interactive features. It is important to engage the user's senses through visuals that can be either animated, videos, or regular images. It is also important to include interactive hover features so the user is guided as to where they can click. Animating the page as the user scrolls is another way to encourage the user to further explore a webpage. It is also important to capture your unique artistic style within your website. Building an interactive website is also a great way to deliver educational information, since it allows the user to engage deeper with the content and creates a memorable experience.

Mate, Anna. “Interactive Websites: Improve Your Site’s Engagement.” UX Studio, 2019, https://uxstudioteam.com/ux-blog/interactive-websites/.
